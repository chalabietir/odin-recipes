Lasagna

Description

American recipe for lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna layers
  • Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Coocke the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to instructions on package
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest before serving
