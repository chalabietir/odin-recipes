Pasta Carbonara

Description

Traditional italian recipe for pasta carbonara

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Guanciale bacon
  • Pecorino romano cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Fry the bacon for 5 minutes
  2. Cook the pasta for 11 minutes
  3. Mix the pasta with the sauce
  4. Add egg and stir for a minutes
  5. Serve while warm
